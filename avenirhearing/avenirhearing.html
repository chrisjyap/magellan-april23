Welcome to
Avenir Hearing Clinic

Avenir Hearing is a group of comprehensive hearing clinics that provides expert audiological care and supportive services for people affected by hearing loss in New Brunswick. It is fitting that our province's motto is Spem Reduxit: Hope Restored as it reflects the aim of our practice.

Avenir Process

Audiological Evaluation

The first step begins with a proper audiological evaluation. The right hearing aid selection is made once we thoroughly understand your hearing loss and lifestyle needs.

Learn More

Hearing Aid Selection

The next step is to assist you in helping you make the appropriate hearing aid selection according to your technological needs as well as your financial needs.

Custom Fitting Process

Lastly, our audiologists will listen intently to your feedback and then use all of our expertise and knowledge to ensure that your hearing aids are properly adjusted.
